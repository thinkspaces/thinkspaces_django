About

Yale Thinkspace is an online platform for collaboration and innovation. Here you can post your ideas onto the site to connect with people who have the skills to contribute to your project. At Thinkspace we hope to revolutionize creativity and entrepreneurship at Yale by bringing people together who otherwise might not have. Too much of the intellectual capital of Yale goes to waste every year for this very reason. Thinkspace changes the playing field and gives you the opportunity to make your team's vision a reality.
